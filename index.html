About


Since I began programming, I knew it was an ideal profession I wanted to join. However, as soon as I started at university on a games programming course, I have found myself completely falling in love with creating games.

I am most motivated when working on gameplay mechanics and AI.
Although more recently I have begun to learn shaders, as the impact that shaders can have to drastically change the look and feel of a game is mind blowing to me. I also am wanting to look into networking and servers more, as games for my own projects would greatly benefit from online features. Additionally the opportunity to work on multiplayer games would be incredibly exciting.

Placement

Computer Games Programming student in my 2nd year at the University of Gloucestershire. I am currently looking for a placement for the academic year 2017/2018.
